Within IEP, your preferences enable you to customize your IEP display, specify which page displays to you when you open IEP, and select which notifications you receive. After preference selections are made, they are saved as your preferred IEP settings until you modify them.

Access My Preferences

To access My Preferences, click on your avatar / profile picture at the top-right of the screen. From the menu that displays, select My Preferences. You may also select Preferences from the left navigation when you are in the My Work context.

After you have selected My Preferences or Preferences, the Preferences page displays with the following options:

  • Notifications - enables you to customize the notifications displayed in the Notification Center.
  • Appearance - enables you to specify the mode and color theme of your IEP display.
  • On startup - enables you to specify which page displays to you when you open IEP.

Notifications

You may select which notifications display in your Notification Center by toggling the switches shown on this page.

Two notification categories are available within Preferences: GitHub and Pipelines. Within these categories you may refine the notifications displayed to you.

  • All notifications are displayed by default (switches on), but you may toggle off any notifications that you do not wish to view.
  • Each preference section has a category level switch as well as individual switches for the notifications within the category.
  • If you have turned off some notifications and would like to revert to viewing all, click the Reset (view all) button.

Appearance

You may specify your mode and select your color theme from the Appearance page.

Specify your mode

You may choose between Light or Dark mode by selecting the corresponding button.

The selected mode takes effect immediately. You may change the mode at any time.

Select your theme color

The Theme color area displays the color options available. Click on a color to select it.

The selected theme color take effect immediately. You may change your theme color at any time.

On startup

When entering IEP for the first time, the system defaults your startup page to My Work - Dashboard.

Until you select a preference on the On startup page, IEP opens to the last page you viewed.

To modify your On startup preference, select the view you would like to display when you open IEP.

  • Last area viewed displays the IEP area you were last viewing – My Work, Projects, SEAL, Insights, Configuration, or Documentation.
  • **My Work Dashboard** displays https://iep.coredevplatform.prod.aws.jpmchase.net/spaces/my/dashboard.
  • Documentation displays https://iep.coredevplatform.prod.aws.jpmchase.net/iep-docs/.
  • Observability displays https://iep.coredevplatform.prod.aws.jpmchase.net/spaces/seal/observability.

The selected On startup page is saved upon selection and takes effect at the start of your next IEP session.
