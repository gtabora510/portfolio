This guide helps you understand how to navigate the Developer Portal (DP) and use the Context selector to set the lens, or contextual area, through which you view information within DP.

## Primary Navigation

DP has four primary navigation items available at the top of the screen: Context selector, Analytics, Configuration, and Documentation.

### Context Selector

> **Note:** The Context selector is not visible when you are within Analytics, Configuration, or Documentation. If you are in one of these areas and you want to access the Context selector, you must click on the DP logo.

DP has three distinct contextual areas: Work, Projects and SIDs. When visible, the Context selector is located directly beneath the DP logo at the top of the left navigation area. It enables you to choose which contextual area you want to view within DP.

By clicking on the Context selector, you can change the lens with which you view information.

- **Work:** Focuses on your individual work. It aggregates all Projects of which you are a member, helping you plan your day and stay in the "flow" state.
- **Projects:** Provides a complete view of your application, including tools, infrastructure, resources, and hygiene.
- **SIDs:** Enables you to view your application portfolio at the SID level, this contextual area supports the selection of one or more SIDs.

Each of the contextual areas has its own set of secondary navigation options, relevant content, and tasks.

When you click on the Context selector, the contextual area you are in is highlighted. To switch to another contextual area, click on its name and then make any relevant selections, such as SID or Project.

After selecting your contextual area, the Context selector navigation item and the secondary navigation options update to show relevant content and tasks.

### Analytics

The Analyticss navigation item allows you to view project and team analytics, metrics, reporting, and more. The information displayed within Analyticss does not directly correlate to the Context selector selection. Rather, you can filter the information shown.

### Configuration

The Configuration navigation item allows you to define rules, policies, and standards for apps, LOB level, and the firm.

### Documentation

The Documentation navigation item provides you with access to technical documentation for ACME-approved products and tooling.

## Global Utilities

The utility bar displays at the top right of the DP screen. It is visible regardless of the contextual area you are in and the items on the utility bar do not change with the contextual area selected.

- **Global search:** Use this to search for information across the portal.
- **Quick actions:** Enables access to the items listed.
- **Help:** Accesses the Help Center.
- **Notification center:** Displays important notifications.
- **Profile icon:** Allows you to set your display preferences or view your profile.
